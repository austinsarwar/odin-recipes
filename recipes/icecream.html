Homemade Ice Cream

Vanilla ice cream delight

Ice cream is the perfect treat and there is nothing better than Homemade vanilla ice cream when your craving a sweet treat. Just relax and we will guide you on this journey of making the best ice cream you will ever have.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir sugar, cream, and milk into pan over low heat until sugar is dissolved. Heat just until mix is hot and a small ring of foam appears around the edge.
  2. Transfer cream mixture to pourable container like a measuring cup and stir in vanilla extract. Chill throughly for atleast 2 hours.
  3. Pour ice cream mix into ice cream maker and churn for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. WHen ice cream is softly frozen serve and enjoy!
